**What is the daily transfer limit?**
Your daily transfer limit is $10,000.

**How do I check my account balance?**
You can check your account balance by typing "check balance" or "what is my balance".

**How do I reset my password?**
You can reset your password by visiting the bank's website and clicking on the "Forgot Password" link.

**Are there any fees for international transfers?**
Yes, international transfers have a flat fee of $25 per transaction.

**How can I order a new checkbook?**
You can order a new checkbook through our mobile app or by visiting any of our branches.

**What are the bank's operating hours?**
Our branches are open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
